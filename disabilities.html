Disabilities

A disability is any condition of the body or the mind (impairment) that makes it more difficult for the person with the condition to do certain activities. Many disabilities are hidden so you never know who has a disability.

Our Plans

Empower

We want to empower people with disabilities by setting an example. Our Disabilities Officer has a disability and had to relearn many things that many people take for granted. Many of our Student Officers have hidden disabilities.

Accessibility

We plan to make all campuses and the services Furness College provides more accessible to people with disabilities.

Raise Awareness

We plan to raise awareness of disabilities.
