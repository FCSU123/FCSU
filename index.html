About Us

The FCSU (Furness College Student Union) is an organisation that is run by students for students. We stand up for student values in a variety of different ways.

This includes representing all students across all campuses by attending governors meetings and working closely with Student Reps. While the Student Reps are part of the college the Student Union is independent however we do receive support and funding from the college.

Another way we stand up for student values is by supporting students. This can include support with their own disabilities or wider issues such as the environment. We support a number of charities who can support students along with offering support on the website and relevant officers.

The Student Union organises many clubs and events throughout the year. Any upcoming clubs or events will be posted to this website and our social media channels along with information on how to get involved.

The FCSU is part of the National Union of Students (NUS) that is made up of over 600 Student Unions and 7 million students. Anyone can be a leader within the Student Union and all students across all campuses are automatically a member of the Student Union

Awareness

As a result of recent events in the local area such as the Knife Angel and rising knife crime it is important to raise awareness of this issue.

The college provides sanitary products to many vulnerable students who need them, many of whom are embarrassed over the situation. That is why it is important people do not misuse these sanitary products as many students rely on them for basic dignity.
