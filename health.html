Mental Health

Mental health includes our psychological, emotional and social well-being. It affects how we feel, think and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, make choices and relate to others. Many people suffer from mental health issues in Furness College so it is important to be supportive and kind.

Our Plans

Self Harm Boxes

We provide self harm boxes where people can get support including websites and alcohol wipes. These boxes can be found in the Student Union area at Furness College.

Clubs

We have clubs and anyone is free to come along to take their mind off things.

Support and Information

Samaritans

Samaritans are available to call free at any time and will listen to your troubles. Call 116 123 or follow the link.

Opa Geibel

Opa Geibel is the local Chaplin and can talk to you about any problems and it does not matter what religion you are.

Contact Number: 07903791612

NHS

The NHS provides an urgent mental health helpline.
