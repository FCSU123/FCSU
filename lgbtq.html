L G B T Q +

LGBTQ+ stands for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer (or sometimes questioning) and the plus represents other identiifes. The first four letters of the acronym have been used since the 1990s but in recent years more letters have been added for more representation.

Lesbian is when a woman is only attracted to women.

Gay is when a man is only attracted to men however it can also be used to refer to lesbians.

Bisexual is when someone is attracted to both genders.

Transgender is when someone identifies as a different gender to the one they were born as.

Queer is an uberablle term. Questioning is when someone is unsure of their identity or sexual orientation.

The plus represents people who are not covered by the other five initials.
