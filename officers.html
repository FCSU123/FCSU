Vice President (Rating Lane)

Varenka T Briggs

Email:40019228@student.furness.ac.uk

I am Varenka T Briggs and I am a student at Barrow Sixth Form College Rating Lane. I took the role of Vice President as I wanted to be a voice for people with disabilties and inspire them to realise that they can achieve whatever they want to achieve. I want to encourage people that their disability, be it mental or physical is not the deciding factor in how successful they will be in life and that a disabiltiy is something that should be viewed positively in society.

I study A-level Law, English Language and History. Outside of college I am working towards achieving my grade 8 singing exam. I sing for venues for example in the Pandemic I sang for Abbey View nursing home. I really enjoy spending time playing netball with a few students from college. Being Vice President for me ment I have the opportunity to set up a social group and a small studios group in college in order to help students and create somewhere people can voice their opinions and grow in confidence. This is a role I feel passionately about and I hope in reading this I have inspired you.
