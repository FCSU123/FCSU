Disabilities Officer

Cavan Armstrong

Email: 40021550@student.furness.ac.uk

My name is Cavan Armstrong. I am 18 years old. I am the Disabilities of the Student Union. I am currently studying Catering and Hospitality. Unfortunately, when I was four I was involved in an accident, which left me with brain injury. I had to learn how to do everything again. It left me with a left side weakness and the loss of my peripheral vision and although I find some things hard, I always try my best.

In my role as Disabilities Officer I think I could bring forward some good ideas for fundraising and the social events. As I have a disability I know how hard it can be, even for those students that do not have a disability, I know how hard it is coming to a new place. Just a friendly face can make all the difference.
